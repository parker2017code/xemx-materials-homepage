# Literature-Derived Positioning

This preview should keep the hierarchy below unless later public-launch review changes the commercial scope.

xemX is best understood as an experimental materials search company, not a generic AI materials company, not a coatings shop, and not a full manufacturing or validation provider.

Strong public foundation:

> xemX helps R&D teams test many real material variations, measure which regions work, and narrow candidates before expensive validation.

## Core

- Combinatorial thin-film materials libraries.
- High-throughput characterization.
- Measured composition-property maps.
- Data-guided iteration.

Plain website translation:

> Create physical material libraries where composition changes across a wafer or sample set, measure many points, and return maps showing which regions deserve validation.

## Strong Adjacent Core

- Localized electrochemical screening of material libraries.
- SDC and SECCM-style local electrochemistry where it fits the project.
- Catalyst, electrode, and electrochemical surface questions.

Plain website translation:

> Local electrochemical screening can compare activity, stability, or surface response across catalyst, electrode, interface, or corrosion-relevant material libraries.

## Application Examples

- Electrocatalysts.
- Water electrolysis and HER / OER / ORR-style electrochemical materials.
- High-entropy alloys and complex solid solutions.
- Oxide and nitride thin-film libraries.
- Magnetic thin films.
- Shape-memory and ferromagnetic shape-memory alloy directions where commercially relevant.
- Semiconductor-relevant thin films.
- Photoelectrochemical and optical thin films.
- Electrical, mechanical, optical, magnetic, and local electrochemical screening.

CO2 electroreduction and battery SEI or battery-interface screening are confirm-before-public claims. Keep them out of the homepage unless Lars/Sven approve them for public use.

## Not Homepage Core

- Broad production coating scale-up.
- Generic custom material solutions.
- Generic AI materials platform.
- Full device validation.
- Full battery cell validation.
- Full semiconductor integration.
- Full manufacturing transfer.

## Methods To Surface On Detail Pages

- Magnetron co-sputtering and reactive co-sputtering.
- 100 mm wafer library design and composition gradients.
- EDX / WDX composition mapping.
- Automated XRD and XCA-assisted phase identification.
- VAE-style XRD novelty detection where useful.
- Four-point probe electrical mapping.
- Nanoindentation.
- Optical / UV-VIS screening.
- MOKE magnetic screening.
- Scanning Droplet Cell.
- SECCM or long-range SECCM for higher-resolution local electrochemistry.
- Active learning and Gaussian-process measurement selection.
- Deposition source permutation strategy for larger multielement search spaces.

## Copy Rule

The site should be broader only in this sense:

> This experimental campaign method applies to many material questions.

It should not imply:

> xemX does everything from material idea to final product approval.

Use concrete campaign language: real samples, material libraries, measured points, local electrochemical screening, composition-property maps, candidate regions, and customer validation.
