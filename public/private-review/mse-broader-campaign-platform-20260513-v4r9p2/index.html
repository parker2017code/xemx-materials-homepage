Sample plan

Measurement

Decision

Private review standard

Every campaign claim should name the material question, sample plan, measured output, and next validation step.

Use: composition range, wafer position, XRD map, resistivity data, validation sample, decision rule.

Cut: broad innovation copy that does not change what an R&D reviewer can inspect.

Boundary: this private preview can be sharper than the public homepage; do not infer public-site copy from it.
